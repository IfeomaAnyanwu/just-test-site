MDCN Mandate

To regulate the practice of Medicine, Dentistry and Alternative Medicine and also ensure discipline among medical practitioners, dental surgeons and Alternative Medicine practitioners within Nigeria.

Our Mission

To regulate the practice of Medicine, Dentistry and Alternative Medicine in the most efficient manner that safeguards best healthcare delivery for Nigerians.

Our Vision

To be the foremost professional regulatory body in Nigeria.
